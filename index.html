Reachy Mini Home Assistant Voice Assistant

Control your Home Assistant with voice using Reachy Mini robot

What is this?

This app transforms your Reachy Mini robot into a voice assistant for Home Assistant. Speak to Reachy Mini, and it will control your smart home devices through Home Assistant's voice commands. Reachy Mini provides visual feedback with head movements and antenna animations while listening and responding.

Installation

Install from Reachy Mini Dashboard:

1

Open Reachy Mini Dashboard

Navigate to the Applications section in your browser

2

Show Community Apps

Check the "Show community apps" checkbox to see third-party applications

3

Find and Install

Search for "Reachy Mini Home Assistant Voice Assistant" and click Install

4

Connect to Home Assistant

The app will be automatically discovered by Home Assistant via mDNS
